Why We're Trusted for UPSC

”TEJAS IAS ACADEMY has been established with a vision to become a one-stop center of excellence for all career initiation programs leading to prestigious jobs in various administrative departments of Central and State Governments. We specialize in and provide the best coaching in Chhattisgarh for UPSC and CGPSC civil services exam preparations. Additionally, we offer top-notch coaching services for SSC, Bank, and Railway recruitment exams.’’

OUR FOCUS

Our aim is to offer top-quality education at affordable fees to students in resource-limited areas such as small towns, cities, and surrounding rural regions. This ensures that students from these areas have equal opportunities to pursue their dreams.
